Frequently Asked Questions

How can I schedule a campus tour?

Campus tours are offered Monday through Friday at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM, and on select Saturdays at 10:00 AM. To schedule a tour, please contact our Admissions Office at (123) 456-7891 or fill out the campus visit request form on our website.

What are the application deadlines?

Early Decision: November 1
Regular Decision: January 15
Transfer Students (Fall): March 1
Transfer Students (Spring): October 15

How do I apply for financial aid?

All students seeking financial aid must complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA). Our school code is 001234. Additionally, some scholarships may require separate applications. Visit our Financial Aid Office or contact them at finaid@horizoncollege.edu for more information.

Is on-campus housing available?

Yes, we offer on-campus housing options for students. Freshman students are guaranteed housing if they apply by the May 1 deadline. For more information about housing options, costs, and the application process, please contact our Housing Office at housing@horizoncollege.edu.
