Welcome to the CHARUSAT Library

Our state-of-the-art library serves as a hub for learning, research, and intellectual exploration. With over 100,000 physical volumes, extensive digital resources, and dedicated spaces for study and collaboration, our library supports the academic needs of all students and faculty members.

The library is staffed by professional librarians who provide expert assistance with research, citation, and information literacy. We offer workshops, orientation sessions, and personalized consultations to help you make the most of our resources.

Study Spaces

Quiet Study Area

Our designated quiet study areas provide a peaceful environment for focused individual work. Features comfortable seating, natural lighting, and power outlets at every desk.

Location: 2nd Floor, North Wing

Capacity: 120 seats

Collaboration Zones

Designed for group projects and collaborative learning, these zones feature movable furniture, whiteboards, and technology for sharing and presenting ideas.

Location: 1st Floor, East Wing

Capacity: 10 rooms (6-8 students each)

Computer Lab

Our computer lab provides access to high-performance computers with specialized software for research, data analysis, design, and other academic purposes.

Location: 1st Floor, South Wing

Capacity: 50 workstations
