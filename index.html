About Horizon College

Founded in 1995, Horizon College has been a pioneer in higher education for over 25 years. Our institution is committed to providing a nurturing environment where students can grow academically, professionally, and personally.

With state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated faculty, and innovative teaching methodologies, we prepare our students to meet the challenges of tomorrow and become future leaders.
