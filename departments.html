Department of Computer Science

Our Computer Science department is dedicated to providing a comprehensive education in theoretical and applied computer science. The department focuses on research in artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, software engineering, and human-computer interaction.

Students gain hands-on experience through various projects, internships, and collaborations with industry partners. The department is equipped with state-of-the-art computer labs, research facilities, and specialized software.

  • Cutting-edge curriculum aligned with industry needs
  • Experienced faculty with industry and research backgrounds
  • Modern computer labs and research centers
  • Industry partnerships for internships and job placements

Department of Physics

The Physics Department at Horizon College is renowned for its excellence in teaching and research. We offer programs that cover classical mechanics, quantum physics, relativity, astrophysics, and particle physics.

Our department houses advanced laboratories, an observatory, and specialized equipment for various experiments. Students work alongside faculty on research projects that contribute to the field's advancement.

  • Comprehensive programs in theoretical and applied physics
  • Advanced research laboratories and observatory
  • Collaboration opportunities with renowned research institutions
  • Regular seminars and workshops by visiting scientists

Department of Business Administration

The Business Administration Department prepares students for leadership roles in the global business environment. Our curriculum covers management, finance, marketing, accounting, entrepreneurship, and international business.

The department maintains strong connections with the business community, providing students with networking opportunities, guest lectures, and real-world case studies. Our business incubator supports student startups and innovations.

  • Industry-aligned curriculum with emphasis on practical skills
  • Business simulation labs and case study approach
  • Entrepreneurship incubator for student startups
  • Corporate partnerships for internships and placements

Department of Literature

The Literature Department offers programs that explore classical and contemporary literature from around the world. Students develop critical thinking, analytical writing, and interpretive skills while studying various literary genres and periods.

Our department hosts literary events, poetry readings, and writing workshops. The department's literary journal provides students with opportunities to publish their creative works and gain editorial experience.

  • Diverse courses covering global literary traditions
  • Creative writing workshops and literary events
  • Department literary journal for student publications
  • Literary research opportunities and conferences

Department of Chemistry

The Chemistry Department provides a strong foundation in organic, inorganic, physical, and analytical chemistry. Our programs emphasize laboratory skills, research methods, and practical applications in various industries.

The department features modern laboratories with advanced analytical instruments and research facilities. Students participate in research projects that address current challenges in environmental chemistry, materials science, and medicinal chemistry.

  • Comprehensive theoretical and practical chemistry education
  • State-of-the-art laboratories with modern instrumentation
  • Research opportunities in various chemistry specializations
  • Industry collaborations for applied research projects
